**Requirement:**
- Enforce strict password rules: Min 8 chars, 1 Uppercase, 1 Lowercase, 1 Number, 1 Special Char.
**UI/UX:**
- Add a criteria list below the password input:
  - `<li id="rule-length">En az 8 karakter</li>`
  - `<li id="rule-upper">Bir büyük harf (A-Z)</li>`
  - `<li id="rule-lower">Bir küçük harf (a-z)</li>`
  - `<li id="rule-number">Bir rakam (0-9)</li>`
